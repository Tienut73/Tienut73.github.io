Cycle the Continental Divide

40th Annual Bike Race April 27, 2019

This race will cover 40 miles. One mile for every year our doors have been open to the public. The race is set to begin at 7:30am and expected to end by 4pm. The route will take participants over ridges, past lakes and down steep hills along connected trails. The cost to enter is $35 per person. BYOB (Bring your own bike.) A helmet is also required. Water will be provided throughout the course, but participants are encouraged to bring their own supply of water. Also consider sunscreen. Avoid wearing loose-fiting clothing.
