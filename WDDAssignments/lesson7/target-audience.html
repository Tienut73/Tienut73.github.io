Target Audience

Joggers

Whether you're training for a marathon, or just trying to lose the few extra pounds you put on during Quarantine, our site has what you need!

- Our forecasts give you up to the minute weather conditions to help you better plan your runs.

- We also provide information on hiking trails and nature areas. We'll warn you of potential muddy trails, high winds, and other adverse conditions.

Families

Before planning your trip to the beach, consult our handy site with regularly updated reports on beach conditions.

- Our site has lots to offer families. Including coloring pages for the kids to print out!

- We have reviews of all the parks in the area. You can search for key features, like a sun covering or access to a pool.

- We work with the local school district to provide up to the minute information regarding school closings due to weather.
