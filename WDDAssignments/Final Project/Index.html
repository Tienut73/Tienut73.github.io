Welcome to The Mountain Spoke! Your one stop bike shop, located in beautiful Bozeman, MT. Home of the coolest trails North West of Atlanta!

Some of the features included on our new site:

  • The Adventure Tours page details daily guided bicycle adventures with information about the length, required skill level, cost and location of the tours.
  • The Event page highlights our annual "Cycle the Continental Divide" event with information about the dates, route, costs and basic requirements. Anyone interested in participating is encouraged to fill out the registration form. (Please include your full name, email, phone, date of birth, home state or country, and any special accommodations/comments.)
  • Our Sales page offers great deals on new and used bikes available for purchase in store.
  • Our Service page provides information regarding availble options for tune ups and repairs.
  • The Contact page contains our location as well as phone and email information.
