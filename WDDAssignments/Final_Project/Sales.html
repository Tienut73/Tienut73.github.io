52" Pennyfarthing - Black

  • $1,600
  • While this machine harks back to the days of the 1880s, when these were popular, it is brought right up to date with the use of modern materials and manufacturing techniques.
  • The frame is made from CrMo and has a moveable rear fork to allow you to change the front fork rake for different riding preferences. As well as the standard double step which is ideal for coasting there is an additional lower step that can be set to the left or right to help mounting. There is even a hole on the front fork to house spare spokes.

Triton Pro Three Wheeled Red Cruiser

  • $400
  • For the distinguished rider comes a most extraordinary ride. The Triton Pro has all of the qualities you look for in a leisure cycle with the low, rugged chassis of something sportier. A dual-joystick steering system and free-wheel mechanism offers smooth maneuverability and gentle wrist, elbow and hand pressure while the ergonomic recumbent seat adds minimal pressure to your back. Suitable for riders with a range of heights, the Mobo Triton Pro offers unprecedented stability with its low center of gravity that's perfect for riding at the beach, achieving a brisk workout and for leisure rides.
  • Tire type: Inflatable rubber tires. Seat height: 12.5 inches above the ground. Cushioned seat. Safety flag included. Recommended riding area: Flat and smooth surfaces.

26" Tandem Bike - Blue

  • $300
  • Two extra large and comfortable cruiser saddles are mounted on a stylish HI-Ten cruiser frame, with easy reach handlebars and fun bright colors. The frame and the whitewall tires together present a colorful look to the tandem bicycle.
