Welcome to Scoots! Your one stop scooter rental shop, located in beautiful Cozumel, Mexico. Home of the coolest off road trails South of Tijuana!

Honda Metro Scooter

  • 3 hour rental: $20
  • Honda Metro Scooter is a time-saver with timeless style and all the modern innovations.
  • It starts with one of Honda’s famously reliable four-stroke engines, a no-shift automatic transmission, and remarkable fuel efficiency.
  • There’s also an electric starter, underseat storage and way more. Take a ride and you’ll see how the Metro Scooter can add some time, gas money, and fun, back into your day.

Honda Dio Scooter

  • 3 hour rental: $30
  • Honda has thrown in a host of new features on the Dio BS6.
  • The most significant of the lot are fully-digital instrumentation and an external fuel-filler.
  • It retains its LED headlight but now also gets an engine kill switch, side-stand cut-off and extra storage on the back of the apron.

Honda PCX150 Scooter

  • 3 hour rental: $40
  • Honda’s metro-tastic PCX150 scooter was on the receiving end of an upgrade last year.
  • It included a facelift from stem to stern that further polishes its ’luxe metropolitan looks to bring more of the swank and swagger associated with the marque, and it comes paired with a more voluminous underseat storage area to increase its ’commuterability’.

Honda Pioneer ATV

  • 3 hour rental: $50
  • At 50-inches wide, it comfortably seats two, so you and a friend can explore any trail, even the ones with width restrictions.
  • And it also fits in a full-sized pickup bed.
  • It’s loaded with features like automatic transmission with AT/MT modes, and a powerful engine that delivers plenty of low-RPM torque.

Jeep Wrangler -4 door with a/c

  • 3 hour rental: $70
  • With the same rugged, distinctively retro look and similar off-road capability, the five-seat Wrangler Unlimited offers a longer wheelbase for easier rear-seat access and more room once you’re back there.
  • A 2018 redesign addressed many Wrangler drawbacks, improving on-road handling and ride comfort while bringing a quieter, more civilized interior and added technology features.

Jeep Wrangler -2 door

  • 3 hour rental: $60
  • All Jeep Wranglers have the same standard engine, a 2.0L Turbo with eTorque.
  • This engine is a mild hybrid that runs steady and smooth on trails while also giving you the benefit of an increased fuel economy without compromising the Wrangler’s performance.
